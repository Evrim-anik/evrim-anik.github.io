I am a quantitative health researcher/ health data scientist based in Leeds. I hold an MSc from the University of Nottingham and a PhD from the University of Leeds (Leeds Institute of Health Sciences).

My work focuses on analysing routinely collected healthcare data to study long-term conditions, health outcomes, and service use.

## Research Focus
- Analysis of health data across multiple long-term conditions
- Mental health, including depression and PTSD
- Intensive care and hospital datasets (including PICU data)
- Real-world evidence in healthcare research
- Observational studies and clinical outcomes research
- Evidence synthesis and meta-analysis
- Health inequalities

## Methods
- Data wrangling and integration of complex health datasets
- Statistical modelling for clinical and observational data
- Analysis of longitudinal and routinely collected health data
- Systematic reviews and meta-analysis
- Reproducible research workflows

I also have a background in mixed methods research and clinical trials.

This site contains my publications, research code, and CV.
